🌊 Ocean View Resort – Reservation System Guide

This guide helps new staff members learn how to use the reservation management system.

1. Logging In

Enter your username and password on the login screen. Contact your administrator if you don't have credentials. Default credentials: admin / Admin@123 (change immediately after first login).

2. Creating a New Reservation

  1. Click New Reservation in the sidebar.
  2. Fill in the guest's full name, contact number, and optional email/address.
  3. Select an available room from the dropdown.
  4. Choose check-in and check-out dates. The cost preview will update automatically.
  5. Enter the number of guests (must not exceed room capacity).
  6. Add any special requests if needed.
  7. Click Confirm Reservation. Note down the reservation number.

3. Viewing a Reservation

Go to View Reservation, enter the reservation number, and click Search. All booking details will be displayed.

4. Generating a Bill

Go to Billing, enter the reservation number, and click Generate/View Bill. An invoice will be displayed that can be printed using the Print button.

5. Cancelling a Reservation

Open the reservation via View Reservation, then click the Cancel button. Only CONFIRMED reservations can be cancelled. The room will be freed automatically.
